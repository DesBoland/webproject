Course details

Front Nine

1



Description: A good drive down the left hand side of the fairway gives a good view of the green. A narrow fairway approach leaves a difficult second shot to a reasonably receptive green. A testing hole without a doubt.
Par: 4
Distance: 395 yards

2



Description: A straight drive out between the Dunes is required for this daunting tee shot. Favour the right half of the fairway on your second approach shot. The approach to this green privides one of golf's most spectacular vistas with breathtaking views.
Par: 3
Distance: 295 yards

3



Description: This is a good par 3 which requires careful club selection depending on the breeze. Any approach shot hit too deep into the green will leave a difficult putt if the pin is near the front of the green..
Par: 3
Distance: 278 yards

4



Description: A straight drive is required on this tough par 5. Try to keep the second shot up left as this offers the best angle to approach this relatively small two-tiered green.
Par: 5
Distance: 425 yards

5



Description: A good drive down the left hand side of the fairway gives a good view of the green but beware of the left bunker which is 250 yards to take out of play. You are left with a difficult second shot to a well guarded green. A testing hole without a doubt.
Par: 3
Distance: 250 yards

6



Description: Favour the right side of the fairway as all the trouble is down the left. Avoid the cross bunkers which cover the fairway from 100 yards out. This green which is protected by a deep valley, is notoriously difficult to find from any range, so always leave a full shot.
Par: 4
Distance: 356 yards

7



Description: A good drive down the left hand side of the fairway gives a good view of the green but beware of the left bunker which is 250 yards to take out of play. You are left with a difficult second shot to a well guarded green. A testing hole without a doubt.
Par: 4
Distance: 345 yards

8



Description: A classic links par 3 of 170 yards. The tee shot from an elevated tee must carry the three bunkers which are short of this gently sloping green.
Par: 3
Distance: 170 yards

9



Description: A superbly shaped par 4 along the Scurmore beach. The fairway is narrow so a well hit drive is required. The second shot is made difficult by the cross bunkers which protect the green.
Par: 4
Distance: 425 yards

Back Nine

10



Description: A straight forward par 4 with a marvellous view of the Moy Estuary. Keep the drive between the mounds that border both sides of the fairway. This will leave a second shot to a two-tier green protected by two deep bunkers.
Par: 4
Distance: 455 yards

11



Description: There is no future in missing the green to the right as a deep ravine awaits leaving a near impossible chance to chip and putt for par. This two-tier green can make the putting extremely difficult, as there is a step which is + 19 yards to carry if the pin is at the back of the green.
Par: 5
Distance: 485 yards

12



Description: It is 232 yards to carry fairway straight line to the green but it is also 232 yards to run out of fairway up the right hand side, so take care. Your second shot needs to be precise in order to find the green which has been cut out of the sand dune on your left.
Par: 3
Distance: 232 yards

13



Description: A good drive over the white stone normally leaves a short shot to this well protected green. One of the few genuine birdie chances but one wayward shot makes par a struggle. Safe play is 200 yards off tee leaving about 100 yards yo hole.
Par: 4
Distance: 310 yards

14



Description: Not the longest of par fives but one which must not be under estimated. Try to keep the second shot up the left hand side for the best view of the green, which slopes from back to front.
Par: 5
Distance: 425 yards

15



Description: A challenging par 4. The drive needs to be kept down the right hand side for the best view of the green which slopes from back to front.
Par: 4
Distance: 421 yards

16



Description: A tough but straight forward par 5 with keeping the ball on the fairway a must. The second shot should favour the right hand side for the best approach as club selection is everything for this green which is wide but very shallow.
Par: 5
Distance: 535 yards

17



Description: Just like the 16th at Sawgrass except more natural. This is 140 yards of terror especially when the wind blows. So take aim, take care and good luck and if all fails, enjoy the view.
Par: 3
Distance: 140 yards

18



Description: Big hitters should bear in mind that the bunkers are 255 yards to reach and 290 yards to carry all traps left and right from medal tees. Beware of out of bounds all along the left.
Par: 5
Distance: 525 yards
